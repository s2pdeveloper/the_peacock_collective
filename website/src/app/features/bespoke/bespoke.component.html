When we decided to launch The Peacock Collective, one of the questions that often came to mind was - “What ticks us?”. After countless market visits, picking one-of-a-kind fabrics, and traveling around India to pick the choicest laces and embellishments, our light-bulb moment was realizing that “solving problems” ticks us! "Bespoke - By, You", is our little design hub where your dream attire becomes a reality, crafted with passion and precision. We believe that clothing is more than just fabric and thread; it's a reflection of your personality, your aspirations, and your unique style. That's why, we're not just tailors; we're creators, collaborators, and problem-solvers dedicated to bringing your vision to life

We are committed to inclusivity in design, and ensure that the outfit fits you, rather than the other way around. Imagine a wardrobe where every piece tells a story, where every stitch is a testament to your individuality. With over 25 years of collective experience in bespoke tailoring, our team of karigars combine craftsmanship with creativity to deliver garments that are not only impeccably tailored but also imbued with your personal touch.

“When I was building out my wedding trousseau, I ventured into every market and handloom store I possibly could. Picked out pearls for my sangeet outfit, and found the perfect organza dupatta to be dyed in the shade of my choice. I truly enjoyed the process and watched every bit of my outfit come together. That was when I realized that this journey is truly precious. As a bride, it meant the world to me, and I want to extend that feeling to you, through this brand. Your story deserves to be told through impeccable craftsmanship and uncompromising quality. Together, let's create something extraordinary, just for you.”

We believe that every client deserves to be an integral part of the creative process. From the initial consultation to the final fitting, we work closely with you, listening to your ideas, understanding your preferences, and translating them into reality. We pride ourselves in our meticulous attention to detail, choosing the finest fabrics and embellishments and creating garments that look and feel luxurious. Whether you're envisioning a timeless trousseau piece or a show-stopping ensemble for a special occasion, our team is dedicated to making your sartorial dreams come true.

At The Peacock Collective, we celebrate individuality. No two clients are alike, and neither are our creations. Whether you prefer classic elegance, avant-garde innovation, or something uniquely in between, our skilled artisans are here to tailor a design that speaks to your style and sensibility. We pride ourselves on solving design challenges that mass-manufactured clothing simply cannot address, ensuring that your bespoke piece fits flawlessly and functions beautifully. We invite you to immerse yourself in the journey of creating something truly extraordinary, where every decision is made with care and consideration for your vision.
