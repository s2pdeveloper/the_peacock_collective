Shipping & Delivery

Shipping & Delivery Policy

We ship domestically within India via DTDC & Bluedart, and internationally via DHL, FedEx, and Aramex. Since we have limited stock and operations in Canada, we ship within Canada using Canada Post.

Shipping rates will be automatically calculated on the website upon placing your order.

Order Status & Tracking

After placing your order, you will receive an email confirmation. For customized orders, the process with my karigars (craftsmen) involves purchasing raw materials and providing them with your order details, which takes approximately 3 – 4 weeks for product creation. Our team conducts a thorough quality check before dispatch.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email confirmation with the tracking number. You can track the delivery status with our logistics partners.

For expedited delivery requests, kindly email me at sukanya@peacockcollective.in, and I will do my best to accommodate.

Taxes, Duties & Tariffs

For international orders, you are responsible for paying all import taxes, duties, and tariffs as per your country’s laws to clear customs. We strive to include these charges at checkout, but this may not always be feasible. Please review your cart for a breakdown of applicable taxes, duties, and charges.

Mode of Payments

We accept payments via most Indian and International credit and debit cards. Indian customers can also use NetBanking.

Due to challenges with Indian payment gateways processing international transactions, success rates can be low. If you encounter issues, please email me at sukanya@peacockcollective.in, and I can provide my bank details for alternative payment options.

Since we are fairly new in these operations, we truly appreciate your patience as we work towards streamlining our processes.
